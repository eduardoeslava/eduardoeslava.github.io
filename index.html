Insigths

  • The bar chart allow locate easily wich was the political division with bigger population enrolled in some basic level of education in colombia
  • Summarize by department and municipality allow a comparative perspective about behavior of population in colombia at 2015 and a distribution of locations used by enrolled students .

What

This dataset presents the following information at the municipality level and that reflects the existing student population as well as educational establishments:

  • Preschool, Basic and Middle School Registration
  • Educational establishments of Preschool, Basic and MiddlE
  • Official Teachers of Preschool, Basic and Media Education
  • Higher education enrollment for the semesters of the year. It differs from the data set "Statistics of Higher Education" in that the latter presents the total statistical information of each validity while this set reports the registration registered per semester.

Why

  • DISCOVER -> SIMILARITIES : Find a little set of political entities and show a representation that allow discover the differences or similarities about population enrolled in some program of education.
  • QUERY -> SUMMARIZE : Allow locate the information related with how is distributed the people that was enrolled represented by physical buildings and their distribution in a municipality level.
